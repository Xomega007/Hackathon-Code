Emeset

Description

Emeset 4 Tablet is an antiemetic medication. It works by blocking the action of a chemical messenger (serotonin) in the brain that may cause nausea and vomiting during anti-cancer treatment (chemotherapy) or after surgery.

Uses

Treatment of Nausea and Vomiting

Side Effects

Constipation, Diarrhea, Fatigue and Headache
