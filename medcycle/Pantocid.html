Pantocid

Description

Pantocid 40 tablet is an acid-reducing medicine. It is used for the treatment of ulcers, heartburn, acid reflux disease and acidity problem. It contains pantoprazole as its active ingredient. An ulce is a sore in the lining of your stomach or intestine that is caused due to digestive acid present in your stomach. It is characterised by burning pain. Pantoprazole reduces the amount of acid produced in the stomach. Thus, prevents ulcers and reduces the backflow of acid that causes heartburn and acidity problem.

Uses

Treatment of Heartburn Treatment of Gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux) Treatment of Peptic ulcer disease

Side Effects

Pantocid Tablet 15's may have common side effects like headache, diarrhoea, nausea, abdominal pain, vomiting, flatulence, dizziness, and arthralgia (joint pain). These side effects are temporary and may get resolved after some time.
