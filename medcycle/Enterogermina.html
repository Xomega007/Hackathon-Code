Enterogermina

Description

Enterogermina Suspension 5 ml belongs to the group of medicines called probiotics used to treat imbalance/alterations of intestinal bacterial flora occurring due to diarrhoea or during the course of therapy with medicines such as antibiotics and chemotherapy. It also helps in the absorption of nutrients, correction of dysvitaminosis. Enterogermina Suspension 5 ml benefits people experiencing gastrointestinal problems such as flatulence and irregular bowel movements.

Uses

Diarrhoea

Side Effects

• Gas
• Bloating
