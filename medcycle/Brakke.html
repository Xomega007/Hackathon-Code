Brakke

Description

Brakke Tablet is a combination of two antibiotics. It is used in the treatment of bacterial and parasitic infections. It effectively treats a wide range of bacterial infections that may occur in the teeth, lungs, gastrointestinal infections, urinary and genital tract.

Uses

Treatment of Bacterial & parasitic infections

Side Effects

Nausea, Vomiting, Dizziness, Headache, Insomnia (difficulty in sleeping), Itching, Vaginal inflammation and Diarrhea
